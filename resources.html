Resources

Mayo: mayoclinic.org
- A resource from reknowned health institution Mayo Clinic addressing toddlers' mental health, the importance of affection, and how to instill discipline during a period in your child's life when they're having a difficult time managing their emotions.

KidsHealth: kidshealth.org
- Concise webpage with useful tips and information regarding your toddler's physical activity and the things you can do to get involved with them to keep them active and having fun.

Stanford: stanfordchildrens.org
- Reliable, in-depth information on the challenges faced when trying to ensure your toddler is eating properly, and invaluable tips and tricks on overcoming these obstacles.
