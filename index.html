Home

Welcome!

Welcome to Thoughts4Tots, a resource for anyone raising, or who will be raising in the future, a toddler or toddlers! This website contains helpful knowledge and tips, collected from various established and trusted sources, in one neat space.

Objective

Our objective at Thoughts4Tots is to provide confidence for indivduals, and couples, who are new to, or otherwise unfamiliar with, the particular challenges in raising toddlers! We hope you find this website useful, and that you leave an even better caretaker for your little one(s) than you already are. Taking it upon yourself to do research is a positive first step!

Navigation

Up there at the top of this page, you'll see the navigation bar. Go ahead and explore rest of the site! Under health you'll find information regarding your toddler's physical health, specifically some fun things you can do to keep him or her physically active and suggestions for how to get them to eat (should they be stubborn *gasp!*), along with nutritional data. Mosey on over to the well-being page to find a discussion on your child's mental health, developmental progress, and how you can play your part! Finally the resources page provides external links to trustworthy sources, a citation of the sources we've used here, and a free pamphlet that we can send you in the mail with more useful tips and info.
